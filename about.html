Who we are?

Restore Mission is a Canadian non-profit guided by the Canada Non-Profit Act and Canada Revenue Agency compliance requirements. We hold dearly our core values of Accountability, Compassion, Excellence, Integrity and Service. Our board and executive team are strongly committed to these values.

We work to restore hope to the vulnerable and the weak in society by providing free education to the children and economically empowering the adults through training. We also provide food and clothing through a free lunch program such that no child goes hungry while learning.

Above all, we bring in the love of Christ as we hope that through the gospel, they are totally transformed and become a positive influence on their world.
