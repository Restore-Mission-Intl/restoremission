    IDP Camp in Benue State - Nigeria

    Since 2013 till date, Benue State in Nigeria has witnessed incessant attacks from armed Fulani herdsmen which, has affected several communities in 20 of the 23 local government areas of the State.

    These invasions have resulted in the massacre of thousands of people, wholesale destruction of communities and total obliteration of economic and cultural assets.

    There are currently over 1.5 million internally displaced persons living across 8 IDP (Internally Displaced Persons) camps within the state.

    An estimated 45% of the IDPs are children many of whom have either been out of school for at least four years or attained school age but were never enrolled within the last four to eight years.

    Over 44,000 children living in the IDP camps are currently reported to be malnourished.
